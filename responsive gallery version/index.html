ABOUT ME

Hi, I'm Lisa and I am currently living in Japan. During the past semester, I studied Informatics at Kyoto University. Here, I got the chance to get to know daily life for someone who lives in Japan, I got to experience student life and the rural area of Uji. I loved studying, but of course I also traveled a lot. In my blog I want to share everything I did, I learned and that I think might be useful for others trying to pursuit a similar path! Do check out pictures, blog posts, maps and everything else I shared. Feel free to visit my social media sites or to contact me!
